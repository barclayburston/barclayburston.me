What can I do for you?

I design & build bespoke front end user experiences. I am completely self taught and am looking to expand on my portfolio by creating beautiful websites for people and businesses.

I work responsively, making sure all of my designs work across every screen size & shape in the world. This includes all mobile platforms such as iPhones and Android devices. To do this I use Bulma as my weapon of choice for front end styling.

What can you do for me?

As my wild career in website creation is still taking off, I would greatly appreciate the opportunity to create a beautiful website for you and display it in my portfolio.

In return I will delve deep into your imagination and convert that to a functional space on the Internet where you can showcase anything you want!

As this is my career I am kickstarting, I would like to make a living from it to help support my bearded dragon and buy coffee. Therefore I will be charging a small fee to create websites to begin with. If you get in touch with me, we can discuss this further.

What can I not do (yet)?

Unfortunately I am not what the industry calls a 'full stack developer'. This means that I cannot make large, complex websites. Unless I have another back end developer to work alongside with, this is simply not possible. I am learning how to work all of the back end of a website, so this might be an option in the future.

I do not work with Wordpress or Bootstrap. I like to create bespoke user experiences using Bulma as a foundation. This framework allows me to create custom designs easily and is a breeze to work with.
